Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 21, 2026

1. Introduction

Soni Digital LLC ("we", "our", or "us") operates the PickWho mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our app. This policy applies to users in the United States and Canada.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your name, email address, and profile information.
  • Username: The display name you choose for the app.
  • Pick History: The sports predictions you make within the app.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers for push notifications.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve PickWho
  • Process your picks and track contest standings
  • Display leaderboards and user rankings
  • Send push notifications about contests, results, and updates
  • Communicate with you about prizes and important updates
  • Monitor and analyze usage trends to improve the app
  • Prevent fraud and ensure fair play

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Public Leaderboards: Your username and pick performance may be displayed on public leaderboards within the app.
  • Service Providers: We work with third-party services (such as Firebase and Google Cloud) to operate the app.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.
  • With Your Consent: We may share information with your explicit permission.

5. Advertising

PickWho displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain information about your device and app usage to serve relevant ads. You can learn more about Google's advertising practices and opt-out options at Google's Ads Settings.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information through app settings
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of push notifications through your device settings
  • Request a copy of your data

8a. Additional Rights for Canadian Users (PIPEDA)

If you are a resident of Canada, you have additional rights under the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and applicable provincial privacy laws:

  • Consent: We will obtain your meaningful consent before collecting, using, or disclosing your personal information, except where permitted by law.
  • Access: You may request access to your personal information held by us and information about how it has been used and disclosed.
  • Correction: You may request correction of your personal information if you believe it is inaccurate or incomplete.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: You may withdraw your consent to our collection, use, or disclosure of your personal information at any time, subject to legal or contractual restrictions.
  • Complaint: You have the right to file a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada if you believe your privacy rights have been violated.

Cross-Border Data Transfer: Please note that your personal information may be stored and processed in the United States, where our servers and service providers are located. By using PickWho, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States, which may have different data protection laws than Canada.

9. Account Deletion

You can delete your PickWho account and all associated data at any time. Here's how:

  1. Open the PickWho app
  2. Tap the Profile tab at the bottom
  3. Tap the Settings gear icon
  4. Scroll down and tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm your decision when prompted

What gets deleted:

  • Your account credentials and profile information
  • All your picks and pick history
  • Your stats, streaks, and achievements
  • Leaderboard entries and contest participation data
  • Push notification tokens and preferences

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. If you encounter any issues deleting your account, please contact us at info@pickwhoisfree.com and we will process your deletion request within 30 days.

10. Push Notifications

We may send you push notifications about contest updates, pick reminders, results, and prizes. You can opt-out of push notifications at any time through your device settings or the app's notification settings.

11. Children's Privacy

PickWho is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we learn we have collected information from a child under 18, we will delete that information.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy in the app or sending you a notification. Your continued use of PickWho after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
