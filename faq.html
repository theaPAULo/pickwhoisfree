Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about PickWho

Is PickWho gambling?

No. PickWho is a free prediction game for entertainment purposes only. You never deposit money, place wagers, or risk any financial loss. There are no odds, spreads, or parlays. You simply pick which team you think will win.

How do I make picks?

Each week, you'll see upcoming games. Tap on the team you predict will win before the game starts. Once a game begins, that pick is locked and cannot be changed.

How do I win prizes?

Each weekly contest has a Prize (typically an Amazon gift card). The user with the most correct predictions at the end of the contest week wins. Tiebreakers are applied if needed. Prize eligibility, amounts, and distribution are at the sole discretion of Soni Digital LLC.

Is the app really free?

Yes, PickWho is completely free to use. You can make unlimited picks and compete in all contests at no cost. We offer an optional premium subscription that removes ads when saving picks, but this is entirely optional. See the app for current pricing.

How is PickWho funded?

PickWho displays ads when free users save their picks. Ad revenue funds the weekly prizes and app operations. Premium subscribers support the app directly through their subscription.

Can I have multiple accounts?

No. Each user may have only one account. Creating multiple accounts is prohibited and will result in termination of all accounts and disqualification from prizes.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account at any time through Settings > Delete Account in the app. Account deletion is permanent and removes all your data including picks, stats, and leaderboard history.
